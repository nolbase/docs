# Analytics

> Measure what matters across all your campaigns.

**Module slug:** `analytics`
**Category:** Analytics & Intelligence
**URL:** `/platform/analytics`

## Overview

Analytics is NolBase's unified reporting and measurement engine. It aggregates performance data across all your advertising channels, email campaigns, and social media — giving you a single source of truth for marketing performance.

No more logging into five ad managers, exporting CSVs, and building spreadsheets. Analytics does it automatically with real-time dashboards, attribution modeling, and AI-powered insights.

## Key Features

### Cross-Channel Dashboards
See every channel in one unified view:
- Meta Ads, Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads, TikTok Ads
- Email campaigns (via Dispatch)
- Social media engagement
- Website analytics (Google Analytics integration)
- Custom channel tracking

### Custom Report Builder
Build exactly the reports you need:
- Drag-and-drop report builder
- Scheduled email delivery (daily, weekly, monthly)
- PDF, CSV, and spreadsheet export
- White-label options for agencies
- Saved report templates

### Attribution Modeling
Understand which touchpoints drive conversions:
- First-touch attribution
- Last-touch attribution
- Linear attribution
- Time-decay attribution
- Position-based attribution
- Data-driven attribution (AI-powered)

### Real-Time Metrics
Live dashboards that update automatically:
- Ad platform data refreshed every 15 minutes
- Email metrics in near real-time
- Website analytics in near real-time
- Social engagement updated hourly

### Goal Tracking
Set targets and monitor progress:
- Campaign-level goal setting (conversions, ROAS, CPA)
- Progress tracking with visual indicators
- Alert notifications when goals are at risk
- Historical goal performance comparison

### AI-Powered Insights
Let AI surface what matters:
- Anomaly detection (unexpected performance changes)
- Optimization recommendations (budget shifts, audience adjustments)
- Trend identification across campaigns
- Performance prediction based on historical patterns

## How It Works

1. **Connect** — Link your ad accounts, analytics, and email platforms
2. **Dashboard** — Data flows automatically into unified dashboards
3. **Analyze** — Drill into campaigns, channels, segments, and time periods
4. **Report** — Generate and schedule reports for stakeholders
5. **Optimize** — Act on AI recommendations to improve performance

## Platform Integration

- **Publish** → Deployment data flows directly into analytics
- **A/B Tests** → Experiment results feed into performance analysis
- **Budgets** → Spend data correlates with performance for ROI calculation
- **Intelligence** → Market context enriches performance interpretation
- **Automate** → Trigger actions based on performance thresholds
