# Approvals

> Review and approve content before it goes live.

**Module slug:** `gate`
**Category:** Content & Creative
**URL:** `/platform/gate`

## Overview

Approvals ensures every piece of content meets your brand standards, compliance requirements, and quality bar before it reaches your audience. It provides customizable review workflows with multi-stage approvals, inline feedback, and complete audit trails.

Whether you're a two-person team doing quick reviews or an enterprise with legal, brand, and compliance sign-offs, Approvals adapts to your process.

## Key Features

### Multi-Stage Approval Chains
Build sequential or parallel approval stages with clear ownership at each step:
- Define who reviews at each stage (by role, team, or individual)
- Sequential stages (legal → brand → final) or parallel (legal + brand simultaneously)
- Skip logic for low-risk content types
- Escalation paths for overdue reviews

### Role-Based Permissions
Granular access controls for every participant:
- **Reviewer** — Can comment and suggest changes
- **Approver** — Can approve or reject with binding authority
- **Admin** — Can configure workflows, override decisions, manage settings

### Inline Commenting & Markup
Contextual feedback directly on the content:
- Comment on specific text, images, or sections
- Threaded discussions for back-and-forth
- @mentions for team notification
- Resolved/unresolved comment tracking

### Version History & Audit Trail
Every edit and approval decision is logged:
- Full version history with diff view
- Who approved/rejected and when
- Comments and feedback preserved
- Exportable audit logs for compliance reporting

### SLA Tracking with Alerts
Set review deadlines and get notified when they're at risk:
- Per-stage SLA configuration
- Overdue notifications via email and Slack
- Dashboard showing bottlenecks and average review times
- Escalation rules for missed deadlines

### Bulk Review Queue
Efficiently review multiple pieces of content:
- Filterable queue by status, type, campaign, urgency
- Batch approve/reject for similar content
- Priority flagging for urgent reviews

## Workflow Capabilities

- Unlimited approval stages per workflow
- Conditional routing based on content type, campaign, brand, or custom attributes
- External stakeholder review links (no NolBase account required)
- Rejection handling with mandatory feedback
- Re-submission tracking after edits
- Slack integration for real-time notifications

## Compliance & Audit

For regulated industries (finance, healthcare, legal):
- Complete audit trail exportable in standard formats
- Tamper-proof approval records with timestamps
- Role-based access ensuring separation of duties
- Full traceability from draft to published content

## How It Works

1. **Route** — Content from Content Studio or manual upload enters the approval workflow
2. **Review** — Assigned reviewers provide inline feedback and approve or request changes
3. **Iterate** — Creators address feedback and resubmit for review
4. **Approve** — Final approver signs off; content is cleared for deployment
5. **Deploy** — Approved content flows directly to Publish

## Platform Integration

- **Content Studio** → One-click routing from generation to review
- **Publish** → Approved content moves directly to deployment
- **Automate** → Trigger workflows based on approval events
- **Slack** → Real-time notifications for review requests and decisions

## Who It's For

- **Enterprise marketing teams** with structured review processes
- **Regulated industries** requiring compliance documentation and audit trails
- **Agencies** managing client approval flows with external review links
