# Budgets

> Track and optimize your marketing budget.

**Module slug:** `spend`
**Category:** Operations & Delivery
**URL:** `/platform/spend`

## Overview

Budgets gives your marketing team a single, real-time view of where every dollar goes. Allocate budgets by channel, campaign, or team. Track spend against plan. Measure ROI. Get alerts before you overspend.

No more spreadsheets that are outdated the moment you close them. Budgets connects directly to your ad platforms and gives you live spend data with accurate attribution.

## Key Features

### Budget Allocation
Allocate at the level that makes sense for your team:
- Per-channel budgets (Meta, Google, LinkedIn, TikTok)
- Per-campaign budgets
- Per-team or per-department budgets
- Monthly, quarterly, and annual time periods
- Budget rollovers and mid-period reallocations

### Real-Time Spend Tracking
Live dashboards showing where every dollar is going:
- Current spend vs. planned budget
- Pacing indicators (on track, underspending, overspending)
- Daily, weekly, and monthly views
- Trend lines and forecasting

### ROI Calculation
Measure what your spend actually returns:
- ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) per channel and campaign
- CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) tracking
- ROI at campaign, channel, and aggregate level
- Revenue attribution from connected CRM data

### Forecast Modeling
Project future spend based on real data:
- Historical pacing analysis
- Spend projections for the rest of the period
- Scenario modeling (what if we increase Meta spend by 20%?)
- Budget optimization recommendations

### Cross-Channel Spend Overview
One unified view across all channels:
- Meta Ads, Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads, TikTok Ads
- Manual spend entry for offline channels, events, and sponsorships
- Agency fees and tool costs
- Consolidated reporting across all sources

### Overspend Alerts
Prevent budget surprises:
- Configurable alert thresholds (80%, 90%, 100% of budget)
- Instant notifications via email, Slack, and in-app
- Hard caps that can pause campaigns automatically
- Escalation to finance or leadership at critical thresholds

## Budget Features

- Multiple concurrent budgets (monthly ops budget + campaign-specific budgets)
- Budget templates for recurring periods
- Approval workflows for budget changes
- Shareable budget reports (PDF or interactive links)
- Historical comparison (this month vs. last month, this quarter vs. last quarter)

## How It Works

1. **Set** — Define budgets by channel, campaign, or team with time periods
2. **Track** — Real-time dashboards show spend vs. plan
3. **Alert** — Get notified before overspend happens
4. **Optimize** — Use ROI data and forecasts to reallocate budget
5. **Report** — Share budget reports with leadership and finance

## Platform Integration

- **Analytics** → Performance data enables accurate ROI calculation
- **Publish** → Spend data from deployed campaigns flows in automatically
- **Automate** → Trigger alerts or pause campaigns based on budget thresholds
- **Forecasts** → ML models predict optimal budget allocation

## Who It's For

- **Marketing directors** needing complete budget visibility and control
- **Finance teams** overseeing marketing expenditure and ROI
- **Agency account managers** tracking client budgets and demonstrating value
