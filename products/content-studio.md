# Content Studio

> Create high-converting marketing content with AI.

**Module slug:** `forge`
**Category:** Content & Creative
**URL:** `/platform/forge`

## Overview

Content Studio is NolBase's AI-powered content generation engine. It produces ad copy, email sequences, landing pages, social posts, and creative briefs in seconds — using models trained on top-performing marketing patterns. Every piece of content is generated from your brand's voice, strategy, and audience data.

Content Studio doesn't just generate text. It understands your positioning (from Strategy), respects your brand rules, and produces variations ready for testing (via A/B Tests) and deployment (via Publish).

## Key Features

### Multi-Format Generation
Generate content across every marketing format from a single workspace:
- Ad copy (Meta, Google, LinkedIn, TikTok)
- Email sequences and nurture campaigns
- Landing page copy (headlines, body, CTAs)
- Social media posts (platform-optimized)
- Blog outlines and long-form content
- Creative briefs and campaign concepts
- Taglines and slogans
- Product descriptions

### Brand Voice Training
Train AI models on your brand's guidelines, tone of voice, and past content. Every generation sounds like your brand — not generic AI output.
- Upload brand guidelines, style guides, and past content
- Define tone attributes (formal, playful, authoritative, etc.)
- Set terminology rules and vocabulary preferences
- AI adapts to your voice over time

### 30+ Templates
Pre-built templates for every channel and format. Select a template, fill in the brief, and generate production-ready content.

### A/B Copy Variants
Generate multiple copy variations instantly. Test different angles, CTAs, and emotional hooks without writing each one manually.

### SEO Optimization
Built-in SEO tools that suggest keywords, generate meta tags, and score content for search performance.

### Bulk Generation
Generate hundreds of variations in a single batch. Ideal for product catalog copy, localized ad variations, or multi-audience campaigns.

## AI Models

Content Studio uses multiple AI models and selects the best one based on content type:
- **GPT-4 Turbo** — Primary model for most content types
- **Claude Sonnet** — Secondary model for nuanced, long-form content
- Model preferences configurable per workspace

## Language Support

Generate content in 25+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Italian, Dutch, Swedish, and more. Native-quality output — not machine translation.

## How It Works

1. **Brief it** — Select a content type, provide context (product, audience, goal), or let Strategy data auto-fill
2. **Generate** — AI produces multiple variations with different angles and CTAs
3. **Refine** — Edit, regenerate specific sections, or request a different tone
4. **Route** — Send to Approvals for review, or directly to Publish for deployment

## Platform Integration

Content Studio is deeply connected to other NolBase modules:
- **Strategy** → Brand voice, personas, and messaging flow in automatically
- **Approvals** → One-click routing to review workflows
- **Publish** → Deploy approved content directly to channels
- **A/B Tests** → Test generated variants with statistical rigor
- **Analytics** → Track which content performs best and feed learnings back
