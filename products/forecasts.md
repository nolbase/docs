# Forecasts

> Predict campaign performance before you spend a dollar.

**Module slug:** `predict`
**Category:** Analytics & Intelligence
**URL:** `/platform/predict`

## Overview

Forecasts uses machine learning models to predict campaign performance, forecast audience behavior, and optimize budget allocation — before you commit spend. It turns historical data into forward-looking intelligence that helps your team make smarter decisions faster.

Stop reacting to results. Start predicting them.

## Key Features

### Campaign Performance Forecasting
Predict results before launching:
- Expected impressions, clicks, and conversions
- Confidence intervals (best case, expected, worst case)
- Platform-specific predictions (Meta, Google, LinkedIn, TikTok)
- Historical accuracy tracking for model calibration

### Audience Behavior Prediction
Understand what your audience will do next:
- Engagement likelihood scoring
- Churn risk identification
- Purchase propensity modeling
- Content preference prediction

### Budget Allocation Optimization
Maximize ROI with data-driven allocation:
- Optimal spend distribution across channels
- Diminishing returns analysis per channel
- Scenario modeling (what if we shift 20% from Meta to LinkedIn?)
- Incremental ROAS projections

### Creative Fatigue Prediction
Know when to refresh before performance drops:
- Ad fatigue forecasting based on frequency and engagement trends
- Recommended refresh timing
- Creative rotation suggestions
- Historical fatigue patterns by channel and audience

## How It Works

1. **Collect** — Forecasts analyzes your historical campaign data automatically
2. **Model** — ML models build predictions based on your specific data patterns
3. **Predict** — View forecasts for upcoming campaigns and budget scenarios
4. **Decide** — Use predictions to allocate budget and prioritize campaigns
5. **Learn** — Models improve over time as more data accumulates

## Platform Integration

- **Analytics** → Historical performance data trains prediction models
- **Budgets** → Forecast-driven allocation recommendations
- **A/B Tests** → Predict test outcomes and estimate required sample sizes
- **Publish** → Timing recommendations for optimal deployment

## Who It's For

- **Marketing leaders** planning budgets and resource allocation
- **Performance marketers** optimizing spend across channels
- **Growth teams** identifying highest-ROI opportunities before committing resources
