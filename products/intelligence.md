# Intelligence

> Track competitors and market trends in real time.

**Module slug:** `signal`
**Category:** Analytics & Intelligence
**URL:** `/platform/signal`

## Overview

Intelligence is NolBase's market monitoring and competitive intelligence module. It tracks competitor activity, industry trends, and audience sentiment across the web — and surfaces actionable insights so your marketing team can respond faster than the competition.

Stop relying on quarterly competitive reviews. Intelligence gives you a continuous, real-time feed of what's happening in your market.

## Key Features

### Competitor Monitoring
Track your competitors across every public signal:
- Campaign launches and messaging changes
- Pricing updates and new product announcements
- Website changes and landing page updates
- Job postings (hiring signals)
- Patent filings and technology moves
- Social media activity and engagement patterns

### Trend Detection
Identify emerging trends before they go mainstream:
- Industry topic tracking with volume and velocity metrics
- Rising keyword and hashtag detection
- Content format trends (what's working in your space)
- Seasonal pattern recognition

### Sentiment Analysis
Monitor how your brand and competitors are perceived:
- Social media sentiment tracking
- Review and rating monitoring
- Forum and community analysis
- Sentiment trends over time with anomaly detection

### Keyword Tracking
Stay on top of your search landscape:
- Keyword ranking tracking across search engines
- Search volume trend analysis
- Content gap identification
- Competitor keyword overlap analysis

### Custom Alerts
Get notified the moment something important happens:
- Competitor activity triggers
- Brand mention spikes
- Market shift detection
- Configurable alert channels (email, Slack, in-app)

### Automated Intelligence Reports
Regular intelligence briefings delivered automatically:
- Weekly market summaries
- Monthly competitive analysis reports
- Custom report templates
- Shareable with stakeholders (PDF or interactive link)

## Data Sources

Intelligence monitors across multiple signal types:
- Competitor websites and landing pages
- Social media (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Facebook, Instagram)
- News outlets and industry publications
- App store listings and reviews
- Job boards (hiring signals)
- Patent databases
- Search engine results pages

## Data Freshness

- Social media monitoring: Near real-time (under 15 minutes)
- Web crawling: Every 24 hours
- News and publications: Continuous
- Custom alerts: Real-time trigger evaluation

## How It Works

1. **Configure** — Set up competitors, keywords, and topics to monitor
2. **Monitor** — Intelligence continuously scans all configured sources
3. **Analyze** — AI surfaces patterns, anomalies, and actionable insights
4. **Act** — Use insights to inform strategy, content, and campaign decisions
5. **Report** — Share intelligence with stakeholders via automated reports

## Platform Integration

- **Strategy** → Intelligence insights feed directly into strategy updates
- **Content Studio** → Generate content that addresses trending topics and competitor gaps
- **Analytics** → Correlate market events with your campaign performance
- **Automate** → Trigger workflows based on intelligence alerts

## Who It's For

- **Product marketers** tracking competitor positioning and launches
- **Strategy teams** grounding decisions in real data, not guesswork
- **Content strategists** discovering trending topics and content opportunities
