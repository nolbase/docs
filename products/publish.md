# Publish

> Deploy campaigns across every channel from one place.

**Module slug:** `deploy`
**Category:** Operations & Delivery
**URL:** `/platform/deploy`

## Overview

Publish is NolBase's multi-channel deployment engine. It handles creative formatting, scheduling, and publishing across every major advertising platform and social channel — so your team can go live faster without switching between five different dashboards.

One calendar. One workflow. Every channel.

## Key Features

### Social Media Scheduling
Schedule and publish across all major social platforms:
- Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, TikTok
- Platform-specific content previews (pixel-perfect)
- Optimal time suggestions based on audience engagement data
- Bulk scheduling for content series

### Email Campaign Deployment
Send targeted email campaigns through the NolBase platform:
- Segmented sending with personalization
- Scheduling and automated sequences
- A/B subject line testing
- Integration with Dispatch for transactional delivery

### Ad Platform Publishing
Push creatives directly to ad platforms:
- Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)
- Google Ads (Search, Display, YouTube)
- LinkedIn Ads
- TikTok Ads
- Automatic format adaptation for each platform's requirements

### Content Calendar
A unified calendar view across all channels:
- Drag-and-drop rescheduling
- Color-coded by channel, campaign, or status
- Team assignments and ownership
- Campaign-level grouping

### Multi-Workspace Support
Manage multiple brands, clients, or business units:
- Workspace isolation for clean separation
- Per-workspace scheduling and calendars
- Cross-workspace content sharing when needed

### Per-Channel Analytics
Track how each piece of content performs without leaving Publish:
- Engagement metrics by platform
- Click-through rates and conversions
- Audience reach and impressions
- Performance comparison across channels

## Deployment Capabilities

- Automatic format adaptation for each platform's specs
- Content rollback and unpublish capability
- Time zone-aware scheduling for global audiences
- Drafts and content staging before go-live
- Approval status visibility (integrates with Approvals)

## How It Works

1. **Create** — Generate content in Content Studio or upload directly
2. **Schedule** — Pick channels, set dates and times, preview per-platform
3. **Review** — Route through Approvals if required
4. **Deploy** — Content goes live automatically at the scheduled time
5. **Measure** — Track performance in-platform or via Analytics

## Platform Integration

- **Content Studio** → Publish AI-generated content directly
- **Approvals** → Only deploy content that's been approved
- **Analytics** → Performance data flows back automatically
- **Automate** → Trigger deployments based on workflow rules
- **Audiences** → Target specific segments with each deployment

- **Campaign managers** coordinating multi-channel launches with precision
- **Global teams** scheduling content across time zones and regions
