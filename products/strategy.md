# Strategy

> Build your marketing strategy and messaging framework.

**Module slug:** `frame`
**Category:** Content & Creative
**URL:** `/platform/frame`

## Overview

Strategy is where your marketing foundation lives. It helps you document personas, positioning, value propositions, objections, and use cases in structured formats that both your team and NolBase's AI can use.

When you define your strategy in Strategy, that context flows directly into Content Studio, Approvals, and every other module. Your AI-generated content sounds like your brand because it starts from your strategy — not a blank prompt.

## Key Features

### Positioning Canvas
Map your market position with a structured framework:
- Category definition and market context
- Target audience and ideal customer profile
- Key differentiators and competitive advantages
- Core value proposition
- Proof points and evidence

### Messaging Hierarchy Builder
Create layered messaging frameworks from brand pillars down to feature-specific proof points:
- Brand-level messaging (mission, vision, promise)
- Product-level messaging (value props, benefits)
- Feature-level messaging (specific proof points, technical details)
- Audience-specific variations

### Persona Profiles
Build detailed buyer personas with structured data:
- Demographics and firmographics
- Pain points and challenges
- Goals and desired outcomes
- Objections and concerns
- Preferred channels and content types
- Decision-making criteria

### Competitive Positioning Maps
Visualize where you stand relative to competitors:
- Feature comparison matrices
- Positioning maps on custom axes
- Competitive messaging analysis
- White space identification

### Campaign Brief Generator
Auto-generate campaign briefs from your strategy data. Select a persona, a product, and a goal — Strategy assembles the brief with relevant messaging, objections to address, and suggested angles.

### Brand Guidelines Hub
Centralize everything that defines your brand:
- Voice and tone guidelines
- Visual standards and design rules
- Terminology and vocabulary preferences
- Do's and don'ts for content creation

## How It Works

1. **Define** — Document your personas, positioning, and messaging in structured templates
2. **Organize** — Build a messaging hierarchy from brand level to feature level
3. **Connect** — Strategy data feeds into Content Studio, Approvals, and other modules
4. **Iterate** — Update strategy as you learn from Analytics and Intelligence data

## Platform Integration

- **Content Studio** → Strategy data auto-fills briefs and trains AI on your positioning
- **Intelligence** → Market data and competitor insights inform strategy updates
- **Analytics** → Campaign performance feeds back into strategy refinement
- **Approvals** → Reviewers can check content against strategy guidelines

## Import & Collaboration

- Import existing strategy documents (PDF, DOCX, Google Docs)
- Unlimited personas and messaging frameworks
- Team collaboration with comments, suggestions, and version history
- Share links for external collaborators and agencies
- Full revision history with rollback

## Who It's For

- **Brand strategists** building positioning and messaging from scratch
- **Product marketing managers** documenting and sharing GTM strategy
- **Startup founders** defining their marketing foundation before scaling
