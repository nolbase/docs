# Video

> Create and manage video marketing content at scale.

**Module slug:** `video`
**Category:** Content & Creative

## Overview

Video brings video content creation into the NolBase platform. From short-form social clips to brand videos, create platform-optimized video assets with AI-assisted scripting, template-based editing, and automated formatting for every channel.

## Key Features

### AI-Assisted Video Creation
Let AI help with the creative process:
- Script generation from campaign briefs and product data
- Storyboard suggestions based on content type and platform
- Voiceover text generation in multiple tones
- Caption and subtitle generation

### Template-Based Editing
Create professional videos without a video team:
- Pre-built templates for Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn
- Drag-and-drop customization with brand assets
- Text overlay and animation presets
- Background music library

### Platform Optimization
Automatic formatting for every platform:
- Instagram Reels (9:16)
- TikTok (9:16)
- YouTube (16:9)
- LinkedIn (1:1, 16:9)
- Facebook Feed (1:1, 4:5)
- Automatic format adaptation from a single source

### Performance Analytics
Track how your videos perform:
- View count and completion rate tracking
- Engagement metrics (likes, shares, comments)
- Drop-off analysis (where viewers stop watching)
- A/B testing for video thumbnails and hooks

## How It Works

1. **Script** — Generate or write video scripts with AI assistance
2. **Create** — Build videos using templates or upload existing footage
3. **Format** — Auto-generate platform-specific versions
4. **Deploy** — Publish directly via Publish module
5. **Measure** — Track performance in Analytics

## Platform Integration

- **Content Studio** → Script and concept generation flows from Content Studio
- **Publish** → Deploy videos across all social channels
- **Analytics** → Video performance feeds into unified dashboards
- **A/B Tests** → Test video thumbnails, hooks, and CTAs
- **Export** → Download in any format and resolution
